The Moscow Peace Treaty is signed, ending the Winter War. Finland cedes territory, including the Karelian Isthmus and parts of Salla and Petsamo, to the Soviet Union.

    Finland

    Finland, like Russia, participated in this war and also has its own culture which makes it a unique country.

  • The landscape is mostly flat, but the main landforms are the Halti mountain, Lemmenjoki national park have a lot of mountains, Utsjoki mountain, and Lapland mountains.

  • Finland counts with a lot of cities, but the largest cities and the most importants are Helsinki and Espoo

    • Finland Culture

  • Finland is known for being the happiest country in the world. Around 75% of its space is covered by forests and it has the largest archipelago in the world, including the largest lakes.
